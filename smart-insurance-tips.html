Smart Insurance Tips

Drive safer. Claim faster. Stay protected.

1. Understand Your Coverage

Know what your policy includes — liability, collision, comprehensive, and add-ons. Read the fine print and ask questions before signing.

2. Drive Defensively

Avoid distractions, follow speed limits, and keep a safe distance. Defensive driving reduces accidents and keeps your premiums low.

3. File Claims Smartly

Document everything — photos, witness info, police reports. Submit your claim quickly and accurately to avoid delays.
